Weird Spaghetti

Description: Really good spaghetti!

Ingredients

  • Red Onion
  • Red Pepper
  • Tomato Paste
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Chop red onion and red bell pepper
  2. Saute until soft
  3. Cook tomato paste
  4. Add ground beef
